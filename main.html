Interstellar follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. The story beautifully blends science, emotion, and the bond between a father and daughter.

Main Actors

The film stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Brand, and Jessica Chastain as Murph. Their performances bring depth and emotion to the story.
